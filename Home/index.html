First Order Ordinary Differential Equation

Theory


First order ordinary differential equation:

Euler's Method:
y2=y1+h*f(x1, y1)

Formulation Second order Runge Kutta Method

K1=h*f(x1,y1)

K2=h*f(x1+h,y1+k1)

K=(k1+k2)/2 y2=y1+k

Formulation Fourth order Runge Kutta Method

K1=h*f(x1,y1)

K2=hf(x1+h/2, y1+k1/2)

K3=hf(x1+h/2, y1+k2/2)

K4=hf(x1+h, y1+k3)

K=(k1+2k2+2k3+k4)/6

y2=y1+k

Second Order Ordinary Differential Equation

Theory


Second order ordinary differential equation:

Formulation for Euler's method

y2=y1+h(x1, y1, z1)

z2=z1+h(x1, y1, z1)

Formulation for RK2 Method

sy1=h(x1, y1, z1)

sz1=h(x1, y1, z1)

sy2=h(x1+h, y1+sy1, z1+sz1)

sz2=h(x1+h, y1+sy1, z1+sz1)

sy=(sy1+sy2)/2

sz=(sz1+sz2)/2

y2=y1+sy

z2=z1+sz
